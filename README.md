# SolGenX

SolGenX is an AI-enabled smart solar energy management system designed for small businesses and homes. It combines dual-axis sun tracking, IoT-based monitoring, and predictive analytics to improve solar energy generation, reduce maintenance issues, and make clean energy more accessible and efficient.

## Project Overview

Many small businesses and households still rely on inefficient energy sources or fixed solar panels that capture only part of available sunlight. SolGenX solves this by turning a traditional solar setup into a smart, self-optimizing system that can track the sun, monitor performance in real time, and support smarter energy use.

## Features

- Dual-axis solar tracking for better sunlight capture.
- AI-powered optimization and predictive maintenance.
- IoT sensors for real-time environmental and system data.
- Remote monitoring through a user-friendly dashboard or app.
- Flexible deployment for homes, small businesses, and rural areas.
- Support for customer-owned and Solar-as-a-Service models.

## How It Works

1. Solar panels track the sun automatically using a dual-axis mechanism.
2. Sensors collect data such as temperature, light, voltage, and current.
3. The SolGenX controller processes the data and adjusts system behavior.
4. AI helps optimize performance and detect potential issues early.
5. Users can monitor the system remotely through an app or dashboard.

The goal of SolGenX is to make solar energy smarter, more efficient, and more affordable for everyday users. It aims to reduce dependence on unreliable grid power and polluting fuels while helping users save money and improve energy independence.
